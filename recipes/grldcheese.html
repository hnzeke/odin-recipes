Grilled Cheese

Description

The recipe calls for Bread, Cheese, and Butter

Ingredients

  • 1/2 slices of Sargento Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 slices of Sprouted Wheat Bread
  • 4 teaspoons of butter

Steps

  1. Use knife to spread butter on 1 side of the 2 slices of bread.
  2. Create sandwich with cheese and bread.
  3. Put sandwich in saucepan and cook each side of the sandwich on medium heat until golden brown. Serve.
