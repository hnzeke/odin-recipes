Pesto

Description

The recipe calls for Basil Pesto, Pasta, Clarified Butter, and Parmesan Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes of Jovial Brown Rice Pasta
  • 2 contaniers of Whole Foods Basil Pesto
  • 2 containers of Whole Foods Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of Ghee Butter

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil and pour in Brown rice pasta. Cook for 10-15 mins on Medium heat.
  2. Strain pasta. As pasta strains put Clarified Butter and Basil Pesto in saute pan and bring to a medium heat.
  3. As the Butter melts and the Basil Pesto slightly bubbles, add Pasta in saute pan.
  4. Mix until combined and keep on low heat for 3 mins. Serve.
