Mac & Cheese

Description

The recipe calls for Pasta, Butter, and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes of Barilla Al Dente Penne
  • Half a bag of Sargento Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons of Butter

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil and pour in Penne pasta. Cook for 10-15 mins on Medium heat.
  2. Strain pasta. After straining pasta add pasta back into saucepan. Add butter and cheese.
  3. Stir while on medium-high heat until cheese and butter melt and bind with pasta. Serve.
